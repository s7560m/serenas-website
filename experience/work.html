Non-Profit Organization

Starts With One

In the summer of 2016, I took part as the President for a non-profit organization, Starts With One, a student run non-profit that hosts events and fundraisers to raise awareness for poverty in the lower mainland. SWO believes in a holistic approach to the alleviation of poverty, and thus, when combatting Greater Vancouver child poverty, we choose to support both families involved and the schools in service. Currently, Starts With One is focussed upon the development of innovative programs with Sir William Macdonald Elementary and Mom2Mom Child Poverty Initiative Vancouver. Through our impact, SWO has raised over $20,000 to combat local child poverty since 2014. We have collaborated with many other organizations, such as The Indian Umbrella, and have had sponsorships from Blenz Coffee, Vanedge Capital, and OpenRoad Auto Group. As we also believe that equitable education is the foundation to achieve a better society, The One to One Program for academic enhancement was founded in March of 2016.
